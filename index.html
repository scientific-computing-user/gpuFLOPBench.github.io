What the benchmark shows

  • Scale. Hundreds of benchmark source binaries and thousands of kernel-device observations.
  • Hardware spread. Direct comparison across RTX 3080, A10, A100, and H100 GPUs.
  • Measured rooflines. Floating-point arithmetic intensity and FLOP/xtime performance in one public-facing benchmark view.
Benchmark structure

One benchmark, multiple layers of visibility.

gpuFLOPBench is not just a single score. It exposes the benchmark from the inventory level down to exact kernel rows so that category coverage, roofline behavior, and source-level detail stay visible at the same time.
